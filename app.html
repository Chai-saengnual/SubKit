📧 Email Reminders (via EmailJS)

SubKit uses EmailJS to send renewal reminders directly from your browser - no server needed. Create a free account at emailjs.com, then paste your keys below.

📋 EMAILJS TEMPLATE VARIABLES TO USE
{{to_email}} - recipient email
{{sub_name}} - subscription name
{{sub_price}} - price in your display currency
{{sub_orig}} - original price + currency
{{renewal_date}} - renewal date
{{days_left}} - days until renewal
{{sub_emoji}} - subscription emoji
