Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 16, 2026

SubKit is built around a simple principle: your subscription data is yours, and you shouldn't have to share your bank login to track what you pay for. This policy explains exactly what we collect, what we don't, and why.

The short version

  • We do not connect to your bank. Ever. Manual entry only.
  • Free users: all data stays on your device (localStorage). Nothing is sent to our servers.
  • Pro users: your data syncs to our Supabase backend so it works across your devices.
  • We never sell your data. We never share it with advertisers. We never train AI on it.

What we collect

Account information (Pro only)

When you create an account, we collect your email address and a hashed password. We use Supabase Auth for this — we never see your plaintext password.

Subscription data (Pro only)

The subscriptions you add: name, price, currency, billing cycle, next renewal date, category, emoji, optional account label, and any notes. This is what syncs across your devices.

Invoice attachments (Pro only)

PDFs and images you upload as invoice attachments. Stored in Supabase Storage, scoped to your user ID. Free users can't attach files.

Payment information

Payments are handled entirely by Stripe. We never see your card number. We only store a Stripe customer ID and subscription ID on your account so we know whether you're on the Free or Pro plan.

Usage analytics

We do not use third-party analytics, tracking pixels, or session replay tools. The only telemetry is server logs (Vercel + Supabase) for debugging.

What we don't collect

  • Bank credentials, transaction history, or account balances (we don't integrate with banks at all)
  • Device fingerprints, advertising IDs, or cross-site tracking data
  • Location data beyond what your browser sends by default (your IP, which Vercel logs for ~24h)
  • Contacts, photos, microphone, or any other device permissions

Where your data lives

Free users: data lives in your browser's localStorage on the device you set up. Clearing your browser data clears SubKit data. There's no account to delete.

Pro users: data lives in our Supabase project (hosted on AWS, us-east-1 region). We use row-level security so you can only ever see your own rows. Database backups are encrypted at rest.

Reminders (optional)

If you opt in, SubKit can send you renewal reminders via:

  • EmailJS — emails go directly from your browser to your email provider using your own EmailJS account. We don't see the email content.
  • Telegram — messages are sent from your browser to Telegram's API using your own bot token. We don't see the message content.

You can disable reminders at any time in Settings. Disabling deletes your EmailJS / Telegram config from localStorage immediately.

Your rights

You can at any time:

  • Export your data (Settings → Export CSV)
  • Delete your account (Settings → Delete account) — this deletes all your data permanently within 7 days
  • Request a copy of everything we have on you — email privacy@subkit.app and we'll send it within 30 days

Children's privacy

SubKit is not directed at children under 13. We don't knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has created an account, email privacy@subkit.app and we'll delete it.

Changes to this policy

If we change anything material, we'll show an in-app banner before the change takes effect. The "Last updated" date above will reflect the change.

Questions? privacy@subkit.app
