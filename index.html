Your device by default. On the free tier, your subscriptions live in your browser's local storage and never leave your device. Upgrade to Pro and they sync to our Supabase project, encrypted in transit and at rest. You can export and delete at any time from Settings.
You'll see a clear upgrade prompt. Your existing data is safe. You can keep viewing and managing your 5 subscriptions; adding a 6th requires Pro. We don't lock you out or threaten to delete anything.
